Internal Software

What is the OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool?

The OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool is a small internal software application that connects to the OralMorals Pinterest business account using the official Pinterest API. Its purpose is to help manage and optimize advertising for the OralMorals Etsy shop.

Scope
Used only for our own account

The tool manages only our own campaigns and Pins. It is not provided to the public and does not allow external Pinterest users to authenticate or connect.

Function
Ad & Analytics automation

The app can create and update ad campaigns, adjust budgets and bids, and read performance metrics (impressions, clicks, spend) to help us understand which creatives and audiences perform best.

Data handling
No third-party user data

The app does not scrape Pinterest, does not collect personal data from Pinterest users, and does not resell or share Pinterest data with any third parties.

Privacy

Privacy Policy – OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool

Last updated: [Set Date]

1. Overview

This privacy policy applies to the internal software tool referred to as the “OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool” (“the App”). The App is used solely to support advertising and analytics for the OralMorals Etsy shop, which sells hydroxyapatite powder and related materials.

2. What the App does

The App connects to the OralMorals Pinterest business account and is used to:

  • Create and manage ad campaigns, ad groups, and ads for OralMorals Pins
  • Retrieve performance metrics for those ads and Pins (such as impressions, clicks, and spend)
  • Analyze results to improve future ad targeting, budget allocation, and creatives

The App is not a public service or platform. It is an internal tool used only by the shop owner.

3. Data accessed from Pinterest

The App may access the following data from the OralMorals Pinterest business account:

  • Information about OralMorals Pins, boards, campaigns, ad groups, and ads
  • Aggregated performance metrics such as impressions, clicks, click-through rate, and spend

The App does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information about individual Pinterest users. All data is accessed via the official Pinterest API and is limited to what Pinterest provides for the OralMorals business account.

4. How data is used

Any data retrieved via the Pinterest API is used solely for:

  • Monitoring the performance of the OralMorals Pinterest ad campaigns
  • Improving the effectiveness of ads and Pins over time
  • Making informed decisions about ad budgeting and targeting

Data accessed by the App is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

5. Data storage

The App may store limited aggregate analytics data (for example, daily totals of impressions, clicks, and spend for OralMorals ads) in private tools such as spreadsheets or databases, for the sole purpose of tracking performance over time.

No personal identifiers of Pinterest users are stored, and no data from other Pinterest business accounts is collected.

6. Third-party services

The App may be hosted or run on third-party infrastructure or hardware (for example, a private server or cloud provider). These providers may process data on behalf of OralMorals but do not have permission to use Pinterest data for their own purposes.

7. Relationship with Pinterest

The OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially sponsored by Pinterest. Use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest’s own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
