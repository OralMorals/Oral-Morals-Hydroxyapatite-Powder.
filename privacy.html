Privacy Policy – OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool

Last updated:

This privacy policy applies to the internal software application known as the OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool (“the App”). The App is used solely by the owner of the OralMorals Etsy shop to manage and optimize advertising campaigns on their own Pinterest business account.

1. What the App Does

The App connects to the OralMorals Pinterest business account using the official Pinterest API. It is used to:

  • Create and manage ad campaigns for OralMorals product Pins
  • Access advertising analytics such as impressions, clicks, CTR, and spend
  • Optimize budgets, bids, and creatives based on performance data

2. Data Collected

The App only accesses data from the OralMorals Pinterest business account, including:

  • OralMorals Pins, boards, campaigns, ad groups, and ads
  • Aggregated ad performance metrics

The App does not collect personal information about Pinterest users and does not retrieve or analyze third-party Pinterest accounts or content.

3. How Data Is Used

All accessed data is used exclusively to improve the performance of the OralMorals Etsy shop’s advertising. No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties.

4. Data Storage

The App may store limited analytics such as daily impression totals or click counts, used only for internal business analysis. No personal user identifiers are stored.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may run on third-party hosting or hardware. These services only process data on behalf of the shop owner and do not gain permission to use Pinterest data for other purposes.

6. Not Affiliated with Pinterest

The App is an internal tool and is not sponsored, endorsed, or affiliated with Pinterest in any way.
