Privacy Policy – OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool

Last updated:

This privacy policy applies to the internal software tool known as the OralMorals Ads Optimization Tool ("the App"). The App is used exclusively by the owner of the OralMorals Etsy shop to manage and optimize advertising associated with their own Pinterest business account.

1. What the App Does

The App connects to the OralMorals Pinterest business account to:

  • Create and update Pinterest ad campaigns for OralMorals product Pins
  • Retrieve advertising analytics such as impressions, clicks, CTR, and spend
  • Adjust bids, budgets, and creatives to improve performance

2. Data Collected

The App only accesses data from the OralMorals Pinterest business account, including:

  • OralMorals Pins, boards, campaigns, ad groups, and ads
  • Aggregated performance metrics related to those ads

The App does not collect personal information about Pinterest users and does not retrieve or process any data from third-party Pinterest accounts.

3. How Data Is Used

All accessed data is used solely to improve the advertising performance of the OralMorals Etsy shop. No data is shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties.

4. Data Storage

The App may store limited metrics (such as daily impressions or click totals) for internal business analysis. No personal identifiers or third-party data are stored.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may run on third-party hosting or hardware. These third parties only process data on behalf of the shop owner and gain no permission to use Pinterest data for any other purpose.

6. Not Affiliated with Pinterest

The App is an internal tool and is not sponsored, endorsed, or affiliated with Pinterest in any way.
